    About Us

    We’re a small family business based in the beautiful county of Kerry.

    Our services include Window Cleaning, Office Cleaning, PowerWashing, Gutter Cleaning.

    We’ve been around in Killarney since 1996, but have over 30 years experience in the Cleaning Industry.

    Using industry leading techniques along with the latest technology and equipment will help us help you keep you property clean.

    ADVICE

    Although we Clean Stuff! we also offer advice on all sorts of cleaning needs to clients and others. From getting stains out of a carpet to cleaning your own windows we’ll always try and help you out.

    TRUST US?

    Being both trustworthy and honest means that we can provide a first-class service, giving our clients the peace of mind they need. Another benefit for using us is the confidentiality we have towards every one of our customers. We hold keys and alarm codes to most of our clients’ properties.
