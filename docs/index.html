    About Us

    We’re a small family business based in the beautiful county of Kerry.

    Our services include Window Cleaning, Office Cleaning, PowerWashing, Gutter Cleaning.

    We’ve been around in Killarney since 1996, but have over 30 years experience in the Cleaning Industry.

    Using industry leading techniques along with the latest technology and equipment will help us help you keep you property clean.

    ADVICE

    Although we Clean Stuff! we also offer advice on all sorts of cleaning needs to clients and others. From getting stains out of a carpet to cleaning your own windows we’ll always try and help you out.

    TRUST US?

    Being both trustworthy and honest means that we can provide a first-class service, giving our clients the peace of mind they need. Another benefit for using us is the confidentiality we have towards every one of our customers. We hold keys and alarm codes to most of our clients’ properties.

    Services

    Window Cleaning

    Commercial & Residential.
    Inside & Outside.
    Up to 4 stories high, all from the safety of the ground using the latest industry leading techniques. With over 30 years experience in cleaning windows, your glass is in safe hands! Once off, or regular cleaning, we are sure to give you a clear view!

    Office cleaning

    A clean, tidy and sanitised area to work in is very important and we aim to please. We can complete daily or weekly cleans to your office environment ensuring you and your workforce can achieve your potential.

    Gutters emptied

    We can empty your gutters from the safety of the ground using the latest advances in equipment and technology. We inspect each gutter before and after and can provide a free video report if requested.

    Powerwashing

    Using high pressure without chemicals can clean any property. Spring cleans, after tenancy cleans, or just a regular maintenance clean, we have the know how and equipment to get your property clean!

    Frequently Asked Questions

    How much do you charge?

    Loads. But we will work with you and your budget, so give us a call and find out how we can help you today!

    Will you scratch my new glass?

    We will NOT. We use industry leading equipment that ensures clean glass without any damage. With many new options on glass effects and finishes we stay up to date with how to keep it clean. If you have specific requirements, we will work with you on the best options too.

    Do you clean the window frames?

    We clean outside frames included in the price! Inside frames get a wipe down as we go, but we also offer a complete frame restoration service. Give us a call for more info.

    Will you clean the windows after my builder?

    No! But we are happy to recommend other cleaner who specialise in this type of post construction cleaning.
